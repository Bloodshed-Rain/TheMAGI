How It Works
Click a game.
Get coached.
Coach-Clippi parses every replay into 20+ computed stats, detects habits, and sends structured context to an LLM. The coaching is specific to your data — not a template.

FRAME-LEVEL PRECISION

Every analysis includes L-cancel percentage, neutral win rate, openings per kill, damage per opening, recovery success rate, ledge option entropy, OOS option diversity, and character-specific tracking — Fox waveshine sequences, Falcon knee conversions, Peach turnip pull breakdowns, Marth Ken combo detection.

Coaching responses use Melee terminology. No hand-holding. Move names, frame data references, matchup-specific advice.

Free right now · BYOK and local models always supported

SET DETECTION

Auto-groups games against the same opponent within 15 minutes. Full opponent history, searchable by tag or connect code.

HABIT ENTROPY

Shannon entropy on OOS options, ledge getups, and knockdown responses. Coach-Clippi finds your patterns before opponents do.

MULTI-LLM

OpenRouter (full model catalog), Gemini, Claude, GPT-4o, or local models via Ollama/LM Studio. Your key, your choice.

LOCAL-FIRST

SQLite on your machine. SHA-256 dedup. Analysis caching. No server, no account, no telemetry. Your data stays yours.

FILE WATCHER

Point at your Slippi replay folder. New games auto-import as you play. Stop alt-tabbing to import manually.
