Capabilities

Tactical Systems

Every tool a competitive Melee player needs, powered by AI analysis and deep game data extraction from your .slp replays.

AI Coaching

Get per-game tactical analysis from LLMs. Streamed in real-time with clickable timestamps that jump to moments in your replay.

Deep Stats

Neutral win rate, openings per kill, L-cancel rate, edgeguard success, recovery patterns, and 26-character signature stat detection.

Session Tracking

Replays auto-grouped into tournament sets by matchup and time proximity. Track your record, trends, and adaptation across sessions.

Opponent Intel

Deep-dive profiles on every opponent. Win rates, character matchup data, habit patterns, and adaptation analysis across your history.

Trend Analysis

Watch your metrics evolve over time. Identify plateaus, breakthroughs, and regressions across any stat with interactive charts.

Multi-Provider LLM

Works with Gemini, OpenRouter, Anthropic, OpenAI, or local models. Bring your own API key or run fully offline.

How It Works

Three steps from raw .slp files to actionable intelligence.

01

Import

Point MAGI at your Slippi replay folder. It bulk-imports, deduplicates, and extracts 50+ statistics per game.

02

Analyze

AI reads your game data and generates coaching feedback -- habits, punish optimization, recovery patterns, adaptation signals.

03

Improve

Track trends across sessions. Watch your neutral win rate climb, your punish game sharpen, your adaptation accelerate.
