How It Works
Click a game.
Get coached.
Coach-Clippi parses every replay into 50+ computed stats with per-character signature tracking, detects habits, and sends structured context to an LLM. The coaching is specific to your data — not a template.

FRAME-LEVEL PRECISION

Every analysis includes L-cancel percentage, neutral win rate, openings per kill, damage per opening, recovery success rate, ledge option entropy, OOS option diversity, and per-character signature stats for all 26 characters — Fox shine spikes, Falcon gentleman counts, Puff rest setups, ICs wobble detection, Marth Ken combos, and more.

Coaching responses use Melee terminology. No hand-holding. Move names, frame data references, matchup-specific advice.

SET DETECTION

Auto-groups games against the same opponent within 15 minutes. Full opponent history, searchable by tag or connect code.

HABIT ENTROPY

Shannon entropy on OOS options, ledge getups, and knockdown responses. Coach-Clippi finds your patterns before opponents do.

SIMPLE LLM SETUP

Three curated models: DeepSeek V3 (best value), Gemini 2.5 Flash (fast), GPT-4o Mini (reliable). No API key config needed — just pick and go.

_

LOCAL-FIRST

SQLite on your machine. SHA-256 dedup. Analysis caching. No server, no account, no telemetry. Your data stays yours.

>_

FILE WATCHER

Point at your Slippi replay folder. New games auto-import as you play. Stop alt-tabbing to import manually.
